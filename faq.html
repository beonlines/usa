Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about SkillStrike Academy.

What do I need to start training?

A modern web browser (like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari) on a desktop, tablet, or mobile device. No downloads, sign-ups, or installations are required to use our training drills.

How are my analytics tracked?

We use a custom analytics system to track your performance metrics. Your training data is saved in your browser's local storage, giving you full control over your information.

Can I reset my data?

Yes. To clear all saved data, including your analytics and training history, open your browser's developer console and run the command: localStorage.clear(). This will reset your player profile.

How do I use the AI analysis?

Our AI analysis works in real-time during specific drills. It provides on-screen feedback and suggestions to help you improve your technique automatically.

Are the drills free to use?

Yes, SkillStrike Academy's core training drills are completely free. We are committed to providing a high-quality, accessible training experience for everyone without mandatory fees or pay-to-win features.
